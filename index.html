Pat Verga

I'm currently a Research Scientist at Google AI in Pittsburgh . I primarily work on knowledge representation and reasoning in the context of large language models and in particular, retrieval and memory augmented language models.

Previosuly, I received my PhD in computer science from UMass Amherst where I worked in the Information Extraction and Synthesis Laboratory (IESL) with Professor Andrew McCallum. My thesis work was on extracting and representing entities and their relations for constructing embedded knowledge bases.
