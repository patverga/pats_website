Patrick Verga

I'm a PhD candidate at UMass Amherst working in the Information Extraction and Synthesis Laboratory (IESL) with Professor Andrew McCallum. My research is in Information Extraction, Natural Language Processing, and Machine Learning.

Previously at UMass, I recieved my M.S. in Computer Science in Fall 2014 as well as my B.S. in Neuroscience and B.A. in Computer Science in May 2012.

In summer 2016 I was an intern at Google Research working with the Commonsense team on multilingual commonsense knowledge base construction.

In summer 2017 I was a Research Science Intern at the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative working with Meta on constructing bio-medical knowledge graphs.
